Reducing Landfill Waste

By diverting over 42,500 pounds of e-waste from landfills, we've prevented harmful chemicals like lead, mercury, and cadmium from contaminating soil and groundwater in our community.

Our recycling process recovers valuable materials like gold, silver, copper, and palladium that can be reused in new electronics, reducing the need for destructive mining practices.

Community Success Stories

Local Tech High School partnered with us to recycle their old computer lab. We provided educational materials about e-waste and gave students a tour of our facility.

Downtown Business Association collected over 500 devices during their "E-Waste Drive" with our support. We provided free pickups and certificates for tax deductions.
